Gymnastic school from 5 to 14 year-old

A memorable time. I finally became the national champion when 13 as an "underdog".
This was my 4 coaches, and my team the second year at a picnic. Count all of us please! ...because by the time we matured to compete, only 4 of us left with the 4 coaches!

Always catching up!

After leaving my gymnastic school, I joined many other teams: from handball teams, volleyball teams, tennis teams, and a high school singing team. It was the happy old time. I was always at top-performance in speed and catching-up in speech due to years in the gymnastic school of over physical training and under social skills practicing!

I was old enough to wear my traditional outfit.

When I turned to 18, like American celebrating their 21, I went to a photo studio with my first not-in-white Ao Dai. (High school Ao dai uniform is white:). The makeup lady made me looked like 30. However, I was very happy as looked like a "lady" at my 18!
