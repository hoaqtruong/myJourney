I solely created and conducted this program for a psycho-educational group focusing on clients who have issues with self-assurance and socializing skills, for a mental health clinic in San Francisco (RAMS). The group was open for new members to join in at any session. The group-size fluctuated from 5 to 10 participants.

Objectives of the program:

  • Participant is able to reflect on an actual situation experienced during the week and to verbalize his or her sense of self through the experience.
  • Participant discusses one social skill each weeks and meets with group peer(s) outside of the clinic settings once a month.

I also conducted a research on the participants' usage of the internet and out-of-town phone communications during the program.

Training materials:

Weekly handouts, workbooks, powerpoint presentations, motivational cards, and evaluation questionnaire.
(Due to a mix of language capacity of the participants, most of the materials were in both Vietnamese and English; but only one version is presented below (some PDF presentations were converted from PowerPoint files).
