How close is 3D Modeling to reality?

Realistic visualization of a fashion design without even touching a pair of scissors. How?

Standard or customized model/avatar sizing

Professional fashion designers can create and grade a design by the industrial S/M/L standard. Hobbyists can tailor the pattern by their own size.

Burda patterns or paper draping? It's the old-time fashion!

From unfolding a piece of pattern, duplicating and mirroring pieces, matching seam line lengths, positioning button holes or zipper, to printing out the final pattern, it takes a few hours instead of weeks.
