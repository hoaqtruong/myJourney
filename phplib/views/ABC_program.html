This is a 5-day intensive motivational training program from LIB Malaysian training company. Prudential Vietnam bought the franchise in 2001. Entire program was translated and modified to serve the company's sell-force, and later also for the staff. Later, our team developed and conducted a series of motivational courses adjacent to the ABC program. My two main responsibilities in this process are: conducting classes and workshops; designing courses and teaching materials:

Conducting motivational classes and workshops

There were variety of training settings taking place in our series of motivational training programs: Classroom, workshop, self-reflecting, team-building indoor and outdoor activities

Designing courses and teaching materials

Presentations of some courses that I took part in the conceptualizing and designing process, including training aids. (The presentations originally are in Powerpoint)
Since it is a 5-day off-site intensive course, the training materials are a rich collection of a textbook, a workbook, powerpoint presentations, movies, motivational cards, complimentary T-shirt and music CDs, and promotion gifts. I created most of vector graphics. Some photos were also taken by myself.

*Currently, under SALT training and consulting company whose founder was the former leader of our training team, ABC program is widely served many companies in Vietnam.
