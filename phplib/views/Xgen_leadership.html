I created this 1-day course for the staff of DRD, a center for youths with disabilities in Vietnam. Per the manager of the center — Ms Yen Dinh — most staff members graduate from high school, some even from middle school, and have limited interpersonal skills and previous work experience. I conceptualized the course based on 6 components: thinking, listening, speaking, gaining insight, creative thinking, and follow up F.E.E.L.I.N.G. I conducted a 1-day workshop to introduce the concept to DRD team leaders. Later on, I assisted them to develop each component into an individual course by themselves.

My ultimate goal was that the course participants gain a good understanding of the communication process and overcome difficulties in communication due to disability conditions and lack of working experience of the staff members.

Objectives

  • Each participant presents 2 personal experiences related to 6 leadership skills: thinking, listening, speaking, insight, creative thinking, and positive feedback.
  • Each participant completes and executes the 21 days action plan.
  • Participant is able to work in 6 groups to create individual courses about the 6 leadership skills.
